CAMILA CABELLO

Camila's Biography and Music.

Image from RollingStone

The world fell in love with Camila Cabello when she was a member of the hitmaking girl group Fifth Harmony. The singer-songwriter, who was born in Cuba, achieved even greater success with her 2018 solo debut, Camila, which featured the number-one hit "Havana."


While in Fifth Harmony, Cabello began to establish herself as a solo artist with the release of the collaborations "I Know What You Did Last Summer" with Shawn Mendes and "Bad Things" with Machine Gun Kelly, the latter reaching number four on the US Billboard Hot 100. She left the group in late 2016. Her debut studio album, Camila (2018), reached number one on the US Billboard 200.
